Biometric Dungeons and Dragons project tool

This is a University of Melbourne research project that aims to explore how facial recognition is able to enhance the gaming experience of Dungeons and Dragons (D&D). We are researching a tool that personalizes a game character's unique attributes by using a photo of the player.

If you continue, you will be asked about what sort of character you like to play. We will also ask you to upload a photo of yourself, which will be used to develop your custom D&D character. We will keep your photo and the generated character information as part of our research. Please be aware that we will never share your photo and survey results with anyone else.

By clicking "Start the tool!", you consent to participate in this study, as agreed in the attached Consent form and Plain language Statement. Please note that you must be at least 18 years old to participate.
